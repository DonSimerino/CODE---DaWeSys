How does it work?
Everytime you search something on the Internet you might open multiple websites to get desired information. The amound of data those websites collect of you is often not even close to be equal to the use you get from the website. Maybe you were less than a minute on the website or you did not even want to click on it, but it already got information about you. Those can be trackers and trackers follow you through the internet.
With Privacy Labels provided by CODE you don't ever have to worry about trackers- simply take a glimpse on the label next to the website before you click on it. And should you see a red one, you better do not click on it. It's simple like a traffic light!
